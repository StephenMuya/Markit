# Monetization Plan

## Monetization Objective
Reach paid traction quickly with subscription products that deliver local decision advantage.

## Pricing Architecture

### Tier 1: Homebuyer Premium (B2C)
**Who:** Homebuyers and household decision-makers

**Includes:**
- Personalized neighborhood change feed
- Nearby development + delivery alerts
- Plain-language weekly local brief

**Model:** Monthly/annual subscription

### Tier 2: Agent/Broker Pro (B2B Seat)
**Who:** Individual agents and brokerage professionals

**Includes everything in Tier 1, plus:**
- Deal and financing signal feed
- Lead-oriented entity watchlists (developers/owners/lenders)
- Export-friendly summaries for client communication

**Model:** Per-seat pricing with team discounts

### Tier 3: Investor Pro (B2B)
**Who:** Active investors and acquisitions teams

**Includes everything in Tier 2, plus:**
- Distress/risk radar
- Advanced filters for debt and transaction events
- Submarket watchlists and comparables context

**Model:** Higher-value monthly/annual subscription

### Tier 4: Enterprise
**Who:** Brokerages, funds, institutional teams

**Includes:**
- Multi-seat access controls
- Higher usage/API/data export entitlement
- Optional custom geography expansion

## Go-To-Market Sequence
1. Launch in TX + NY with paid self-serve plans
2. Convert active individuals to team seats
3. Expand via referrals, local partners, and enterprise pilots

## Monetization Priorities
1. Alerts and weekly briefing retention loops
2. Persona-specific dashboard clarity
3. Frictionless upgrades from individual to team plans
4. Expansion through geography and feature depth

## KPI Targets (Directionally)
- Trial-to-paid conversion
- MRR growth by persona and market
- Churn and net revenue retention
- Average revenue per user/seat
