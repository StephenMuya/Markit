# Monetization Overview

## Purpose
This document provides a public-facing overview of Markit monetization structure for Texas and New York.

## Commercial Packaging

### Homebuyer Premium
- Neighborhood change summaries
- Development and delivery alerts
- Weekly market brief

### Agent/Broker Pro
- Everything in Homebuyer Premium
- Deals and financing signal feed
- Entity watchlists and export-ready summaries

### Investor Pro
- Everything in Agent/Broker Pro
- Distress/risk signal tracking
- Advanced market filtering and submarket watchlists

### Enterprise
- Multi-user access
- Workflow integrations
- Expanded coverage and custom reporting options

## Notes on Sensitive Commercial Data
Detailed pricing, conversion targets, revenue goals, and internal go-to-market metrics are intentionally kept out of this repository and maintained in internal planning systems.
